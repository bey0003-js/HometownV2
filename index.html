Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

For emergencies, we typically arrive within 30-60 minutes in the Melbourne metro area. Our 24/7 emergency service ensures you're never left without help when you need it most.

Do you charge a call-out fee?

No, we don't charge any call-out fees if we complete the work. We provide upfront pricing before starting any work so there are no surprises.

Are your electricians licensed and insured?

Yes, all our electricians are fully licensed, insured, and police-checked. We maintain the highest standards of safety and professionalism on every job.

Do you provide quotes before starting work?

Absolutely. We always provide detailed quotes before beginning any work. For simple jobs, we can often quote over the phone. For more complex projects, we'll arrange a free on-site assessment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, EFTPOS, direct bank transfer, and cash. For larger jobs, we offer flexible payment plans.

Do you provide a warranty on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee. We provide a 12-month warranty on all labor and use quality parts that come with manufacturer warranties.
