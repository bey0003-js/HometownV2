Warning Signs You Need Power Point Service

Sparking Outlets

Visible sparks when plugging in or unplugging devices indicate dangerous electrical faults.

Hot Outlets

Warm or hot outlets suggest overloading or faulty wiring that needs immediate attention.

Loose Connections

Plugs falling out or loose outlets indicate worn components requiring replacement.

Burn Marks

Discoloration or burn marks around outlets signal serious electrical problems.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many power points can be on one circuit?

According to Australian standards, up to 10 power points can be connected to a single 20-amp circuit. However, the actual number depends on the expected load and usage patterns.

Can I install power points myself?

No, power point installation must be performed by a licensed electrician in Australia. DIY electrical work is illegal and dangerous, potentially causing fires or electrocution.

How long does power point installation take?

Simple power point installation typically takes 1-2 hours per outlet. Complex installations requiring new circuits may take longer depending on accessibility and requirements.

Do you provide warranty on power point work?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive 2-year warranty on all power point installation and repair work, covering both materials and workmanship for your peace of mind.
