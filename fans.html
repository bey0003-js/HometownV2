Frequently Asked Questions

What size ceiling fan do I need?

Fan size depends on room dimensions. Small rooms (up to 10m²) need 36-42" fans, medium rooms (10-15m²) need 44-52" fans, and large rooms (15m²+) need 54" or larger fans.

Can you install a fan where there's no existing wiring?

Yes, our licensed electricians can run new wiring from your switchboard to install fans in any location. We'll assess the best route and provide a detailed quote.

How long does fan installation take?

Standard ceiling fan installation typically takes 1-2 hours. New wiring or complex installations may take longer depending on accessibility and requirements.

Do you provide warranties on fan installations?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties on all installation work and can arrange manufacturer warranties on the fans themselves.
