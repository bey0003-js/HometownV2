Frequently Asked Questions

How long does security system installation take?

Most residential security system installations take 1-2 days, depending on the size of your property and the complexity of the system. Commercial installations may take 2-5 days. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your home or business and always clean up thoroughly after installation.

Do security systems require ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we recommend annual maintenance checks to ensure all components are functioning properly. This includes testing sensors, cameras, and alarms, checking battery backups, and updating software. We offer maintenance plans that include regular servicing and priority support.

Can I monitor my security system from my phone?

Yes, all our modern security systems include mobile applications that allow you to monitor cameras, receive alerts, and control your system remotely. You can arm or disarm the system, check camera feeds, and even integrate with other smart home features from anywhere with an internet connection.

What happens during a power outage?

Our security systems include backup battery power that keeps the system operational during power outages. The backup duration varies by system but typically provides 8-24 hours of continuous operation. For extended protection, we can install systems with generator integration or larger capacity backup power supplies.

Do you offer monitoring services?

We partner with professional monitoring services that provide 24/7 surveillance of your security system. In the event of an alarm, the monitoring center will verify the alert and dispatch emergency services if needed. We can set up self-monitoring options as well, where alerts go directly to your designated contacts.
